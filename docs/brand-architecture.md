# Brand Architecture

## Company

**gntrio** is the parent brand and trading name. It's how the business is introduced verbally and how it appears in legal and formal contexts.

## Two Domains, One Expertise

The business runs two client-facing properties that reflect two sides of the same expertise — advertising and the AI systems built to encode it.

### gntrio.com — Advertising Consulting
Performance marketing and Google Ads management. This is the primary client acquisition channel. Clients are businesses that need expert media buying, campaign management, and advertising strategy.

### gntr.io — AI Development
Custom AI systems, data pipelines, and productised expertise. Clients are businesses that want to systemise and scale — often beyond what manual management can support.

## How They Work Together

The consulting practice is both a revenue stream and a pipeline for the AI work. A client who has been managed for 12–18 months is a natural fit for a productised AI system — they trust the expertise, and the business already understands their operation deeply. That's a warmer conversation than any cold inbound lead.

The AI work, in turn, credentialises the consulting. The portfolio demonstrates that the advertising expertise is deep enough to encode into systems — not just deep enough to run campaigns.

## The Positioning

> You're not an AI developer who happens to know Google Ads.
> You're an advertising expert who builds AI systems to encode that expertise.

That distinction is the competitive moat. Most AI development agencies don't have operating domain knowledge. Most advertising consultants don't build proprietary systems. gntrio sits at the intersection.

## Domain Logic

| Domain | Purpose | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| `gntrio.com` | Advertising consulting, lead gen | Marketing directors, business owners |
| `gntr.io` | AI systems, technical product | Operators ready to scale or productise |

Both properties make the relationship between them explicit. `gntrio.com` surfaces the AI capability as a natural next step. `gntr.io` surfaces the advertising domain experience as proof the systems are built by someone who has done the work.
