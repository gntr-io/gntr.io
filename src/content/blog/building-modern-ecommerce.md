## The Modern E-commerce Landscape

E-commerce has evolved significantly, with users expecting fast, secure, and seamless shopping experiences across all devices.

## Essential Features

- Fast page load times
- Mobile-responsive design
- Secure payment processing
- Inventory management
- Order tracking

## Technology Stack

Choosing the right technology stack is crucial for long-term success and scalability.

### Recommended Stack:

- Frontend: Astro, React, or Next.js
- Backend: Node.js, Python, or Go
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB
- Payments: Stripe or Square

## Security Considerations

Security should be a top priority when handling customer data and payment information.
